Imagery and Photography

Chosen photography will reflect the innovative spirit and professional ethos of eMoney Advisor. Our platform is built for leaders in the financial industry, so imagery will evoke a commitment to and passion for excellence.

Ideal environments will correspond to authentic surroundings for advisors and clients, such as restaurants and golf courses. The people are modern, professional, and present a sense of wealth. Showcasing strong relationships is important.

Imagery Dos

  • Realistic meeting environments such as …
    • Home visits
    • Golf courses
    • Restaurants
    • Teleconferences
    • Making a presentation
  • Both genders
  • Dark colored suits
  • Smart, professional people

Imagery Don'ts

  • Empty offices, empty chairs, empty conference rooms
  • Images that are too "stock-photography"
    • Staged, sterile, cold
    • People looking at the camera and smiling
    • Groups of generic business people
    • Someone in deep thought
  • Too "hip" (20-somethings in a coffee shop)
  • Handshakes
  • Dollar signs, banks
  • < 35 years old
