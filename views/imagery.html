Imagery and Photography

Chosen photography reflects the innovative and professional spirit of eMoney Advisor. Our platform is built for leaders in the financial industry, so imagery will evoke a commitment to and passion for excellence.

There are several imagery and photography themes outlined below. The Imagery Dos and Don'ts correspond to each theme.

See the Palettes page for more information on what Imagery Themes can be used depending on your target audience.

Imagery Dos

  • Realistic meeting environments such as …
    • Home visits
    • Golf courses
    • Restaurants
    • Teleconferences
    • Making a presentation
  • Both genders
  • Dark colored suits
  • Smart, professional people

Imagery Don'ts

  • Empty offices, empty chairs, empty conference rooms
  • Images that are too "stock-photography"
    • Staged, sterile, cold, disconnected
    • People looking at the camera and smiling
    • Groups of generic business people
    • Someone in deep thought
    • Corporate and cheesy
  • Too young, hip, startup, or coffee shop environments
  • Handshakes
  • Dollar signs, banks
  • People younger than about 35 years old

Theme Option 1: Dramatic

Dramatic: White on Dark Background

  • Night time city scapes
  • Matrix/techno/Minority Report feel

Theme Option 2: Light, Metaphoric

Light

  • Clouds
  • Aerial
  • Bridges
  • Abstract Patterns

Theme Option 3: App Showcase

Spacious: Minimal, White Background

This minimal type of environment is perfect for displaying devices with app screenshots.

For in-situ mockups, show on the newest devices in a professional setting. Avoid multiple devices in one realistic setting.

Theme Option 4: Journalistic/Authentic

Journalistic/Authentic

Ideal authentic environments will correspond to real surroundings for advisors and clients:

  • Restaurants
  • Golf courses
  • Giving presentations
  • Home offices
  • Client meetings at home or in an office

Showing strong, genuine relationships is important. The people shown in these pictures are:

  • Modern
  • Professional
  • Knowledgable
  • Tech savvy
  • Present a sense of wealth

Other journalistic imagery approaches include:

  • Lifestyle photos
  • Crowds of people
