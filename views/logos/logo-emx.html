emX Logos

Logo Colors

emX black on white and light backgrounds

emX white on black and dark backgrounds

emX blue on white and light grey backgrounds

The emX logo should appear in black or white on a contrasting background. If the logo is over a midtone or color background, use an all-white “dropped out” version.

The emX logo should not be used over a busy or high-contrast image.

The app should be referred to as emX, with a lower-case e and m.

The blue emX logo is Neutral Blue, #468cc8 . It should be on a white or light grey background (Empty Chart #eee or lighter).

Exclusion Zones

The emX "e" is a unit of measure for spacing around the logo

Logo with proper spacing is free of distraction or clutter

Give the logo space to maximize its visual presence. The minimum space required surrounding the logo is the width of the emX "e".

Logo Variations

emX Pro

emX Select

Variations of the emX logo. The same color and exclusion zone standards apply.

Square Formats

emX Pro, square format

emX Select, square format

For square formats, use this logo variation. The square format is only used with emX Pro or emX Select cannot be used due to space constraints.

Unacceptable Uses

  • Do not distort the logo proportions.
  • Do not apply color to any part of the logo besides #468cc8 .
  • Do not delete, truncate, or otherwise omit any part of the logo.
  • Do not change the case of the logo.
  • Logo should have maximum contrast and visibility.
