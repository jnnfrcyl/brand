Corporate Logo

Logo Colors

eMoney black on white and light backgrounds

eMoney white on black and dark backgrounds

The eMoney corporate logo should appear in black or white on a contrasting background. If the logo is over a midtone or color background, use an all-white “dropped out” version.

The eMoney logo should not be used over a busy or high-contrast image.

The full name of our company is eMoney Advisor, LLC. If no logo is present on the page, then the first or largest reference to the name should receive a TM superscript. The keyboard symbol for this is alt + 0153. Example: eMoney Advisor, LLC™ Once the full name has been established, it is acceptable to refer to the company as eMoney.

Logo Sizes

"Advisor" should not have a cap height smaller than 1/16"

"M" should not be larger than the largest cap height on layout

The logo should not be large on a page, except for Trade Show signage.

"Advisor" may be dropped if the cap height is smaller than 1/16".

Exclusion Zones

The eMoney "e" is a unit of measure for spacing around the logo

Logo with proper spacing is free of distraction or clutter

Give the logo space to maximize its visual presence. The minimum space required surrounding the logo is the width of the eMoney "e".

Square Formats

For square formats like social media avatars and mobile app bookmarks, use this logo variation. The square format "e" is only used when eMoney Advisor cannot be used due to space constraints.

Do not crop the square format close to the "e". Do not change colors.

Unacceptable Uses

  • Do not distort the logo proportions.
  • Do not apply color to any part of the logo.
  • Do not delete, truncate, or otherwise omit any part of the logo.
  • Do not change the case of the logo.
  • Logo should have maximum contrast and visibility.
