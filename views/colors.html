Links
  • Primary Links in main content areas should be "Neutral Blue"
  • Muted Primary Links: "Neutral Blue" at 40% opacity. 100% Opacity on Hover.
  • Secondary links (links in footer, etc) can be the same color as the rest of the text in those sections, with text underline on hover.
Chart Colors

Colors should be used in the order that they are presented in the chart:

  • Chart Blue, Chart Green, Chart Orange, Chart Purple, Chart Yellow
  • Darker Colors: Chart Blue Shade, Chart Green Shade, Chart Orange Shade, Chart Purple Shade, Chart Yellow Shade
  • Lighter ColorsChart Blue Tint, Chart Green Tint, Chart Orange Tint, Chart Purple Tint, Chart Yellow Tint

"Warning Red" should be avoided in charts unless it is representing Expenses, Taxes, or Debt. If the chart has a category for Cash, that should be represented with "Good Green"

Monte Carlo Scores
  • "Good Green" - Scores 82 and above on EMA
  • "Chart Yellow" - Score 70-81 on EMA
  • "Warning Red" - Score 0 to 69 on EMA

*Note: These ranges are configurable on the Office level

Developer Notes

Investments

  • Use Black or White for main investment value, depending on background color.
  • Good Green indicates up or positive change values.
  • Warning Red indicates down or negative change values.

Net Worth

  • Use Black or White for main net worth value, depending on background color.
  • Good Green indicates up or positive change values.
  • Black indicates down or negative change values, not Warning Red.

Asset Allocation

  • These colors are specific and should not be changed.
  • For EMA and most partners, we use a defined list taken from Evestnet's colors with minimal customization.
  • Some partners have custom Asset Allocation colors. Always ask about colors before working on Asset Allocation projects.

Decision Center

  • Good Green indicates any rise or surplus in assets for a given year.
  • Neutral Blue indicates the assets you are projected to have (before and after techniques).
  • DC Grey indicates a loss or shortage in assets for a given year.
