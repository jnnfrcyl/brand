Typography Fonts and sizes

Our default font stack is Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif, with a base font size of 14px and a line height of 1.428.

Headers

Headers should be made with the <h1> - <h6> tags, or the .h1 - .h6 classes. They should be used in a hierachical manner, meaning that things pertaining to an h1 should only use an h2 and things pertaining to the h2 should only use an h3, etc.

The size of the headers are:

  • <h1> is 28px, with a margin-top of 20px and a margin-bottom of 10px
  • <h2> is 25px, with a margin-top of 20px and a margin-bottom of 10px
  • <h3> is 21px, with a margin-top of 20px and a margin-bottom of 10px
  • <h4> is 18px, with a margin-top of 10px and a margin-bottom of 10px
  • <h5> is 14px, with a margin-top of 10px and a margin-bottom of 10px
  • <h6> is 12px, with a margin-top of 10px and a margin-bottom of 10px

The .h1 - .h6 classes have the same font-size, but they do not have a top or bottom margin.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs should be used when you're writing sentences of content that will. We use <p> tags to space one block of content from another. You should not use a <p> tag if you're just writing out a snippet of text or a number.

Paragraphs have a default bottom margin of 10px.

Bold

Use the <strong> tag to make things bold. There are currently no utility classes to make an element bold. Do not use the <b> tag.

Italic

Use the <em> tag or the .text-italic class to make things italic (or give them emphasis). Do not use the <i> tag.

Underline

We currently do not have a utility class to make things underlined. Do not use the <u> tag.
